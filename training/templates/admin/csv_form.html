Please note that existing participants will be removed and replaced with the contents in your CSV. You will see the changes reflected in the form after the data has been successfully uploaded.
The CSV must contain the following elements

  • A heading row, with the names of the columns
  • Organization: Full name of the organization, if the name doesn't match an existing organization, a new one will be created
  • Role: Role of the participants within the organization as indicated in their registration. This field can be left blank
  • Gender: Enter F for Female, M for Male, or leave blank if not specified
  • Country: Country that the participants identified in their registration (usually their country of residence)
  • Presurveycompleted, Postsurveycompleted: Enter <1 to confirm the corresponding survey was filled out. These fields can be left blank
  • Usparticipantstate: State the participant resides in, if the participant is located in the USA. This field can be left blank
